The Awesome Boat

Sebago Lake is the second largest lake in Maine and is known for its landlocked salmon and lake trout fishing. It is also home to smallmouth bass, white perch, and chain pickerel. Sebago Lake is located in southern Maine and is about 30 minutes from Portland. It is a great place to fish and enjoy the outdoors.

Sebago Lake Fishing and Guiding Services offers guided fishing trips on Sebago Lake. We offer half day and full day trips. We can accommodate up to 4 people per trip. We provide all the fishing gear and bait. We also provide snacks and drinks. We can also provide lunch for an additional fee. We can also provide transportation to and from the lake for an additional fee.

Our guide, Glen Gisel, has fished Sebago Lake for more than 30 years and has guided for XX years. He is a licensed Maine Guide and is fully insured. He is also a member of the Maine Professional Guides Association.
