What to Expect on Our Trips

Fishing Charters

Our fishing charters are perfect for anglers of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fisherman, our knowledgeable captains will guide you to the best fishing spots and provide you with top-quality gear. Expect to catch a variety of fish species, enjoy stunning views, and create unforgettable memories on the water.

Food and Drink

I provide water and a lunch. You are welcome to bring alcholo along, but drink responsible.

What to Bring

  • Sunscreen and hat for sun protection
  • Camera to capture the incredible moments
  • Layered clothing to accommodate changing weather conditions
  • Comfortable, non-slip footwear
  • Snacks and beverages (if allowed)
  • Valid fishing license (for fishing charters)

Our trips typically last between 4 to 6 hours, depending on the type of excursion you choose. We provide all the necessary equipment and safety gear, so you can focus on enjoying your time on the water.
