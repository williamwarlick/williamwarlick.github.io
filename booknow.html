Booking Information

For assistance with booking a trip please call or text Glen at (207) 329-2505. Next day trips may be available, please call to inquire! If you prefer to send an e-mail, please use our contact page.

Pricing

ALL PRICES ARE FOR THE BOAT NOT PER PERSON with a maximum of 4 persons per charter. Paypal, MC/Visa/AMEX, Cash and Checks accepted

  • Half day/four (4) hours $450.00 USD
  • Full day/eight (8) hours $700.00 USD
  • Trips can be extended if schedule permits for $80 per hour (maximum of $700)

Provided:

  • All fishing gear and bait (but you can bring your own)
  • Lunch with a full day trip (upon request)

Required at time of trip:

  • A valid Maine Fishing License is required (purchase it here)
  • A completed Hold Harmless agreement is required. You can download a pdf by clicking here and bring a copy the day of trip. Or fill out and submit the form online in advance.

For the best experience, bring these items:

  • Sun glasses & sun screen
  • Jacket/sweatshirt/rain jacket (depending on weather)
  • Any medicine you might need for the day
  • Camera
  • Personal water bottle
