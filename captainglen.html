Captain Glen Gisel

Captain Glen Gisel is a Registered Maine Guide and US Coast Guard master captain, with endorsements in freshwater fishing, tidewater fishing, and recreational guiding. He have been fishing on Sebago Lake + the surrounding area for over 40 years, and have been taking people fishing for over 20 years.

My guiding career started when I was 16 years old during a bass tournament. One of the anglers saw me catching some monsters from shore and asked me to join him , with my knowledge of the lake he won the tournament! Since then, it's been my dream to be a fishing guide. 40 years later, here I am living that dream! I feel blessed to be able to share that dream with hundreds of people from all over the world every year.

It is my goal to provide you a safe, relaxing, and enjoyable trip on some of Maines most beautiful waters. Consider it fishing therapy, a time to step offshore and leave the hustle and stress of life behind.
